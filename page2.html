Interests and Hobbies

Shooting,

I am a collegiate national champion trap shooter and compete regularly in the sport as a member of the Amateur Trap Association. I also occasionally shoot with the NSCA a different league in sporting clays which is another shooting discipline. I have been shooting since I was in the 7th grade and have loved every minute of it. Currently, I am most active with the Texas A&M Trap and Skeet team as well the Texas Trap Association.
